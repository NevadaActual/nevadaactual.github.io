The majority of Lorn's earlier work was self-published, but much of it came under the umbrella of the Self Confidence compilations and were re-released under Wednesday Sound. Wednesday Sound at one point hosted the albums on their website, but the links have since been taken down and only exist on the Internet Archive. These compilations are a huge portion of Lorn's portfolio, including both his self-published work and many many demos, with the full runtime clocking in at just under 6 hours of music. While some saw a remastering, and were released again under RARITIES, the majority only exist in this lower-quality 192 kbps format. Lorn himself in a reddit comment (click) said that "any masters [he] could find (and still liked) were put on RARITIES".
